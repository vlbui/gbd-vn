Figure 2. Age-standardized DALY rate heatmap for the top causes of disability-adjusted life years in Vietnam, 1990-2023.
Rows (n=12) are the union of the ten highest-ranked causes by age-standardized DALY rate in 1990 and in 2023, restricted to GBD level-2 causes and excluding the aggregate groups Communicable / maternal / neonatal / nutritional diseases, Non-communicable diseases, Injuries and All causes.
Rates are age-standardized to the GBD 2010 world standard population and expressed per 100,000 person-years.
Panel A shows absolute DALY rate on a log colour scale (viridis, vmin=10, vmax=5,000 per 100,000); cell labels report the absolute rate. Panel B shows the log₂ ratio of each year's rate to the 1990 rate on a diverging colour scale (RdBu_r, TwoSlopeNorm centred at 0, vmin=−2, vmax=+2); cell labels report the percentage change vs 1990. Hatched cells in Panel B indicate years whose 95% uncertainty interval on the ratio spans 1.0 (no detectable change at α=0.05 under the independent-UI approximation, using rate_low/rate_high_1990 and rate_high/rate_low_1990 as the ratio UI bounds).
Left-margin colour patches indicate the level-1 cause group: CMNN (blue), NCD (red), Injuries (grey).
